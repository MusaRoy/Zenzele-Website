About Us

Zenzele Tyre Services (PTY) LTD was established in 2018 founded by Eric Taba and Lucky Magatsela. Since establishment, we are OTR (Over the road) Tyre services, tyre repair, Strip and fit. Tyre supply and Wheel Rims/Mags supply has become our speciality through tyre services activities. We have managed to offer complementary services such as Battery supply. We are a company committed to exceptional quality and we have made it part of our identity. This is achieved through the dedication of our highly trained and experienced employees who are always motivated.
